## 🧾 Seamless GST-Ready Billing

Cloud ERP simplifies GST compliance with:

- ✔ Instant invoice generation  
- ✔ Auto HSN & SAC code mapping  
- ✔ Automatic CGST, SGST, IGST & cess calculations  
- ✔ Built-in discount & scheme configuration  

Invoices can be shared instantly via PDF, WhatsApp, or email — accelerating payment cycles from days to hours.

The system also supports:

- 🔄 Bank reconciliation  
- 📑 Auto-generated GSTR-1 & GSTR-3B reports  
- 🛡 Audit-ready transaction records  

Businesses report up to **90% fewer billing errors**, allowing finance teams to focus on growth strategy instead of manual corrections.
